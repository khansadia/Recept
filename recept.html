Biryani

Ingredienser

  • 2 cups Basmati- Rice
  • 1 kg chicken
  • Onion 3 large, sliced
  • 1 cup Yoghurt
  • 3G (Ginger,Garlic,Green Chilli)paste 4tsp
  • 1 cup Tomato puree
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • Green coriander leaves 2 tbsp, chopped
  • Water 3 1/2 cups
  • 7 tbsp Oil
  • Salt as required

To cook Follow These Steps

  • Make a mixture with tomato yoghurt, puree, green chilli paste,
    ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, roasted cumin powder,
    turmeric powder, garam masala powder, coriander powder and salt.
  • Take the chicken and marinade it in the same mixture. Let it rest for 3-4 hours.
  • Put oil in a pan, heat it and fry onions till they turn golden brown.

  • Now, to this add the marinated chicken and cook the entire mixture for 10 mints.

  • After that in a pressure cooker, take the rice and add 3 1/2 cups of water to it.
  • Lastly, add the cardamom powder and the chicken pieces, along with the marinade.

  • Mix all the ingredients gently, cover with the Pot and cook for 15 minutes on low heat

  • Your tasty chicken biryani recipe is ready.
